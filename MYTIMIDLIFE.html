MY TIMID LIFE

Childhood Teenage College

Childhood Years

1I was born in Calamba, Laguna, where my life began. 2My childhood was filled with curiosity and simple joys. 3After four years, my family moved to Pangil, Laguna. 4This place became the center of my early memories. 5I spent most days playing with my cousins. 6Those moments made me feel free. 7I was naturally shy and quiet. 8Talking to strangers felt uncomfortable. 9I made only a few close friends. 10Some friendships faded with time. 11I sometimes showed defiance toward my parents. 12Still, I remained respectful most of the time. 13School became an important part of my life. 14Second grade was my academic peak. 15I felt confident and motivated to learn. 16Meeting new classmates later made me anxious. 17The pressure affected my focus. 18I struggled but continued forward. 19Graduating elementary school felt rewarding. 20This prepared me for my teenage years.

Teenage Years

1My teenage years introduced new challenges. 2I met many new people. 3Friendships became more meaningful. 4I explored different places with friends. 5Those memories remain special. 6Life felt simple and fun. 7The pandemic changed everything. 8I became isolated at home. 9Online communication became my routine. 10We slowly lost touch. 11This affected my confidence. 12I struggled to speak clearly. 13Senior high felt overwhelming. 14I often felt nervous. 15I met supportive classmates. 16They helped me improve. 17I learned resilience. 18I regained confidence slowly. 19This stage shaped my character. 20It prepared me for college life.

College Years

1College life was a major adjustment. 2The city felt overwhelming. 3I felt both excited and scared. 4My first day at UE was nerve-racking. 5I knew no one at first. 6The campus felt large. 7The first semester was challenging. 8I often felt unsure. 9The second semester felt lighter. 10I met new friends. 11They made school enjoyable. 12I appreciated the quality of education. 13The professors were helpful. 14The facilities impressed me. 15Escalators and elevators were convenient. 16Food trips became a habit. 17I learned time management. 18I value my education more. 19I aim to pass all subjects. 20College continues to shape my future.
