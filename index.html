About Me

Hi, I'm John Miguel Barrientos Morales. Born in Calamba, Laguna and raised in Pangil, Laguna. I have 2 siblings and I'm the youngest out of the three of us. My mother is a stay at home mom and the one who manages our apartment. My father is an OFW in Abu Dhabi and still currently working until now.
I have 2 siblings which is my big sister the first child, second is my big brother which is the second child, and lastly me, the youngest from the three of us.
